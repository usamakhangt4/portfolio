## Problem

Medicine information is easy to overwhelm users with. A useful interface needs to make search quick, show results clearly, and keep safety, dosage, and warning details readable without pretending to replace professional medical advice.

## Approach

Medipedia uses Next.js and TypeScript to build a focused drug search surface. The UI includes search affordances, loading states, empty states, error handling, and structured content sections so medicine information is easier to scan.

## Outcome

The deployed interface gives users a focused way to search for medicines and review dosage, safety, and warning information in a readable layout. Its copy and presentation keep the product framed as an information tool rather than a source of medical advice.
